NgoroNgoro Crater

Ngorongoro Conservation Area, located in Tanzania, East Africa, encompasses the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its breathtaking landscapes and rich wildlife. This area is a major draw for tourists seeking extraordinary safari experiences and a chance to witness abundant wildlife in its natural habitat.

Why Ngorongoro?

Ngorongoro Conservation Area is a destination that promises an unforgettable blend of natural beauty, incredible wildlife encounters, and cultural immersion. It is an ideal place for those who seek to explore the wonders of the African wilderness and gain a deeper understanding of the delicate balance between humans and nature. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, an adventure seeker, or a culture explorer, Ngorongoro has something exceptional to offer every traveler.

About NgoroNgoro

Ngorongoro Crater is regarded by many as the 8th wonders of the world. Its crater wall is of about 600 meter and it has a floor about 300 square kilometers, the crater floor has a big number of resident animals with exception of few that migrates in and out of the crater like wildebeest and zebra. Ngorongoro crater is among few areas in the World where black rhino are leaving in their natural habitat. You may expect to spot from hippos, zebra, wildebeest, topi, buffalo, elephant, lion, cheetah, leopard, hyenas and other species..
